About These Dashboards

These interactive dashboards present Environmental Impact Assessment findings and humanitarian response strategies for ADRA's projects in Mali, with a focus on the Tominian and San districts.

Developed in partnership with local communities and stakeholders, these tools provide actionable insights for sustainable development and humanitarian assistance.

Key Features:
  • Evidence-based environmental analysis
  • Community-focused solutions
  • Practical implementation guidance
  • Real-time monitoring capabilities
